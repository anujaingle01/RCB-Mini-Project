Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and one of the most prolific batsmen in modern cricket. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he captained the Indian national team across all formats. Known for his aggressive batting style and excellent technique, Kohli has broken numerous records, including being the fastest to reach several milestones in ODIs. He has been awarded the ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times. Beyond cricket, he is an entrepreneur and philanthropist. Married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, they have a daughter named Vamika.
